Break down silos, get a complete view of your customers.

You already have lots of customer data. CRM, product usage, billing, marketing, support... You name it.

But this data is all over the place. And it is in a form that is hardly of any use to understand who your customers really are and what they care about.

MadKudu automatically brings all this data together. You now have a unified view of your customers, understand their journey and know how to improve it.

Pro-actively reach out to customers who need attention.

Asking customers to come back after they decided to leave is like trying to convince your spouse to come back after receiving the divorce papers.

It is much more effective to offer to help before customers say they need attention.

MadKudu analyses all your customer interactions, then finds which ones are having issue and need help. You can now proactively reach out and get those customers back on the success track.

Nurture each customers like if they were your only one.

Every customer is different. They have different needs. They are at different stage of their journey. What works with a customer will not work with another.

MadKudu tells you which behaviors make your customers more engaged.

You can now suggest to each customers their best next step, based on what they have done and what's most relevant to them right now.
